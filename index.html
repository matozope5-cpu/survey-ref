Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member?
Signing up is completely free and easy. Just click the "Get Started" button, fill in your email and create a password. After confirming your email, complete your profile to start receiving relevant surveys.
What if I forgot my password?
If you've forgotten your password, simply click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. We'll send an email with instructions to reset your password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox.
Why am I not receiving any survey invitations?
Survey invitations depend on various factors including your demographic information, profile completeness, and our clients' specific targeting criteria. To increase your chances, ensure your profile is 100% complete and accurate.
How can I withdraw my money?
You can withdraw your earnings once you reach the minimum threshold of Ksh 500. We offer multiple withdrawal methods including M-Pesa, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payments are typically processed within 24-48 hours.
Why am I not qualified for a survey?
Surveys target specific demographics based on our clients' research needs. If you're disqualified, it simply means you don't match the specific criteria for that particular survey. Don't worry – this is normal and happens to everyone.
